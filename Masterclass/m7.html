Module 7: Designing Ads in Canva

Creating Photo and Video Ads with Canva

In this guide, I’ll show you how to create effective photo and video ads using Canva, a powerful tool that is free to use with optional premium features. I’ll take you through the process of setting up your account, using templates, and creating both photo and video ads for your brand.

Getting Started with Canva

To start, you’ll need to sign up for a Canva account. It's simple and free, though Canva also offers a pro version with additional features.

  • Sign Up Process: Go to Google and search for Canva. Sign up easily using your Gmail account.
  • Free and Paid Versions: The free version provides great tools, but the paid Pro version offers access to additional templates and design elements.
  • Canva Templates: Once logged in, you can access numerous templates for different purposes (e.g., YouTube thumbnails, Facebook ads, etc.).

Creating a Simple Photo Ad

Now let’s create a quick photo ad for a Facebook campaign using Canva.

  • Choosing an Ad Type: Search for "Facebook Ad" in the templates section. Canva automatically offers various ad options.
  • Using a Template: Browse through available templates, select one that fits your brand, and start customizing.
  • Premium Features: If you’re on the Pro version, you'll have access to premium templates and design elements like high-quality stock photos and fonts.

Making a Video Ad with Canva

Next, let’s create a video ad. We’ll use a brand’s soda cans (e.g., Olipop) and build a dynamic ad using images and animations in Canva.

  • Uploading Your Assets: Download high-quality product images (like soda cans) from the brand's website, then upload them into Canva using the "Uploads" tab.
  • Adding Your Branding: You can upload logos, create a custom color palette, and add brand-specific fonts using Canva’s "Brand Kit" feature.
  • Choosing a Video Template: Go to the "Video" section and select a blank video template to start your project.
  • Editing the Video: Drag and drop your images into the video editor. Adjust the background, add text, and apply animations to your elements.
  • Customizing Colors and Animations: Use Canva’s auto-color detection feature to match your ad’s background with the colors in your product image. You can also apply text animations (e.g., neon glow) to make the ad more exciting.

Enhancing Your Video Ad

Here’s how you can refine your video ad further:

  • Using Animations: Canva offers a variety of animations. Apply page-level animations or animations to individual elements to make the ad more dynamic.
  • Duplicate Pages for Multiple Products: Save time by duplicating pages in your video ad, and just swap out the product images for other flavors or variations of your product.
  • Adjusting Timing: Set the duration of each frame to optimize pacing, and apply transitions between scenes to keep the viewer engaged.
  • Final Adjustments: Add finishing touches like star effects or a catchy slogan (e.g., "Healthy Meets Delicious") to enhance the branding message.

Exporting and Finalizing Your Ad

Once your video ad is ready, it’s time to download and share it.

  • Download Your Video: When satisfied, download your video as an MP4 file and it’s ready for use in your campaign.
  • Share Your Ad: Use your new ad for social media platforms, websites, or any other marketing channels you’re targeting.

With Canva, you can easily create professional-looking ads for both photo and video formats, all within your browser. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned marketer, Canva makes ad creation simple and accessible!
