Welcome to the fool's journey.

The "fool" is the one that ignores the broad way, the well-trodden path. The fool paves their own way... but it is not free of trials and tribulations.

It is the embracing of the unknown—the fire.

At times it can be scary, but with the right support, it can be a hell of a journey. One that warrants the word living.

This is the goal of every email I send (which will usually be every Saturday), to provide info that either equips you or inspires you to go through this journey.
